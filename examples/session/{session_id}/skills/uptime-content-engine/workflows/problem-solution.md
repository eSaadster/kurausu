# Problem-Solution Headlines Workflow

**Creates hero copy, value propositions, and section headers using Uptime's problem-first approach.**

---

## Purpose

This workflow generates headlines that make fleet managers stop scrolling. The secret: make them feel the problem before you offer the solution. When they nod at the problem, they're ready to hear the answer.

**Use for:**
- Landing page hero sections
- Section headers throughout pages
- Ad headlines (Google, LinkedIn, Meta)
- Email subject lines
- Value proposition statements

---

### Step 1: Understand the Context

### Step 4: Add Subheadline

**Subheadline delivers the solution with proof:**

```
FORMULA:
[Solution name] is the [category reframe] you [emotional hook]. [Speed proof]. [Guarantee].

EXAMPLE:
"Uptime is the CDL Hiring OS you should have had all along. 14-day driver delivery. Guaranteed."
```

**Subheadline variations:**
- Speed-focused: "First driver in 14 days. Guaranteed."
- Ease-focused: "Installs in one day. Runs forever."
- Risk-focused: "No match, no charge."
- Outcome-focused: "Fill your empty seats. Stop the bleeding."

### Step 6: Validate Against Uptime Voice

**Checklist:**

- [ ] **Problem-first?** Does the headline acknowledge pain before promising gain?
- [ ] **Specific?** Are there numbers or concrete claims?
- [ ] **Direct?** No hedging words ("might", "could", "help")?
- [ ] **Punchy?** Short sentences, strong rhythm?
- [ ] **Credible?** Claims are believable and provable?
- [ ] **Actionable?** Clear what to do next?

**Red flags to fix:**
- Starting with "Uptime helps..." â†’ Flip to problem-first
- Vague claims â†’ Add specific numbers
- Long sentences â†’ Break into short punches

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Example | Fix |
|---------|---------|-----|
| Solution-first | "Uptime helps you hire faster" | "Your hiring is broken. Uptime fixes it." |
| Vague claims | "Better hiring outcomes" | "14-day driver delivery. Guaranteed." |
| Passive voice | "Drivers can be found quickly" | "Get your first driver in 14 days" |
| Generic SaaS | "Streamline your recruiting" | "Stop chasing. Build a pipeline." |
| No proof | "The best hiring platform" | "9 days to first hire. 60% lower cost." |
| Weak CTA | "Learn more" | "Get your first driver free" |
