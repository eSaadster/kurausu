## Style Direction

### The 3 Klaus Styles (AI selects based on content)

**1. Sumi-e Ink (Japanese Ink Painting)**
- Expressive black ink brush strokes with varying thickness
- **Hajime** (beginning) strokes thick and loaded with ink
- **Owari** (ending) strokes taper to fine points
- Extensive **MA (é–“)** â€” intentional negative space (zen emptiness)
- Ink wash gradients from deep black to subtle gray
- Organic, flowing linework (NOT geometric)
- Crab Red as HANKO SEAL ACCENT (artist's stamp style)
- Gold Leaf for subtle premium touches
- Use for: abstract concepts, emotional themes, zen metaphors, philosophical content

**2. Japanese Precision (Ligne Claire meets Ukiyo-e)**
- Clean, uniform black outlines of consistent width
- Flat color fills (Crab Red, Gold Leaf, Klaus Off-White)
- NO gradients â€” architectural precision with Japanese aesthetic
- High negative space (zen-like emptiness)
- Woodblock print influence (ukiyo-e)
- Bold, graphic composition
- Use for: technical diagrams, clear explanations, structured content, tutorials

**3. Textural Neobrutalism**
- Fine black cross-hatching and linework for tone/texture
- **Bold 3px borders** on major elements (neobrutalist signature)
- Premium Gold Leaf warmth throughout
- High contrast: black on warm cream
- Old-world archival meets modern brutalism
- More density allowed than other styles
- Use for: premium feel, archival content, gravitas, authority, dense information

### Japanese Brush Stroke Terminology (for Sumi-e prompts)
| Term | Meaning | Usage |
|------|---------|-------|
| **Hajime** | Beginning stroke | Thick, loaded with ink |
| **Owari** | Ending stroke | Tapers to fine point |
| **Fude-atari** | Brush touch | Variation in pressure |
| **Ma (é–“)** | Negative space | Intentional emptiness |
| **Notan** | Light/dark balance | Contrast harmony |

### Style Selection Guidance
| Content Type | Recommended Klaus Style |
|--------------|-------------------------|
| Abstract/emotional concepts | **Sumi-e Ink** |
| Zen/minimalist themes | **Sumi-e Ink** |
| Philosophical content | **Sumi-e Ink** |
| Technical systems/architecture | **Japanese Precision** |
| Clear explanations/tutorials | **Japanese Precision** |
| Structured narratives | **Japanese Precision** |
| Premium/archival content | **Textural Neobrutalism** |
| Gravitas/authority | **Textural Neobrutalism** |
| Dense information | **Textural Neobrutalism** |

### AVOID
- Generic digital illustration aesthetic
- Stock photo / "AI slop" look (too perfect, too shiny, too smooth)
- Inconsistent style mixing within single image
- Photorealistic elements (unless intentional contrast)
- Over-saturated neon colors
- Clip-art or cheap cartoonish aesthetic
- Gradients in Japanese Precision or Neobrutalism styles (only Sumi-e ink washes allowed)
- Cluttered compositions that violate ma (é–“) principle (except Neobrutalism)
